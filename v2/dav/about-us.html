WHO WE ARE

It's a delight to have you here!

Belwet Mind Clinic is an organization that is established based on our passionate love for you. A dream of 2016 came to manifest in 2022. It is so disheartening that a lot of people need therapy but do not know because mental health awareness in Nigeria is still poor.

That is why an average Nigerian sees an individual who wants to consult a mental health therapist as being mad or almost mad. The next offensive slang you hear is yaba left or Sako Sako.

Also, a lot of people fear being stigmatized and guilt-tripped. Nigeria is a very religious Nation and an average Nigerian is always ready to cast the first stone.

We don't just provide therapy in a soothing atmosphere, we are also determined to be an exceptional mental health advocate by creating a series of articles, videos, seminars and lectures on mental health awareness.

We have a new and growing YouTube channel just for you. Belwet mind clinic is built on these four principles-enlighten, communicate, connect and explore. We provide services to children, teens, young adults, old adults, couples, groups, organizations and communities. You can access our exceptional online services from the comfort of your device 24/7.
Temporarily, physical face-to-face services are provided in Kaduna and Abuja, Nigeria.
