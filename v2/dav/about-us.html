WHO WE ARE

It's a delight to have you here!

Belwet Mind Clinic is an organization that is established based on our passionate love for you. A dream of 2016 came to manifest in 2022. It is so disheartening that a lot of people need therapy but do not know because mental health awareness in Nigeria is still poor.

That is why an average Nigerian sees an individual who wants to consult a mental health therapist as being mad or almost mad. The next offensive slang you hear is yaba left or Sako Sako.

Also, a lot of people fear being stigmatized and guilt-tripped. Nigeria is a very religious Nation and an average Nigerian is always ready to cast the first stone.

We don't just provide therapy in a soothing atmosphere, we are also determined to be an exceptional mental health advocate by creating a series of articles, videos, seminars and lectures on mental health awareness.

We have a new and growing YouTube channel just for you. Belwet mind clinic is built on these four principles-enlighten, communicate, connect and explore. We provide services to children, teens, young adults, old adults, couples, groups, organizations and communities. You can access our exceptional online services from the comfort of your device 24/7.
Temporarily, physical face-to-face services are provided in Kaduna and Abuja, Nigeria.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

A growing organization aimed at bringing effective and outstanding quality services for people of all ages and cultures to manage and live above life stresses and mental health challenges. As they maximize their potential for complete well-being and not just the absence of a challenge or psychological disorder.

Vision

To be the most preferred clinic for convenient mental health advocacy, training, therapy and consultation services in Africa and beyond.

Our Service Approach

a

Evidence Focused

We are attentive to our client's improvement and feedback.

b

Convenience

Almost everything ranging from shopping to business is conveniently delivered these days. Why not therapy, coaching, training, and other mental health services?

c

Personalized Profiling

We understand that therapy without clinical and psychological assessment is short-lived. Therefore, in between mind clinics, a psychological assessment precedes our services.

d

Relaxing and Friendly

We believe that clients live to regret and not heal when the service offered is rigid and guilt-trapped. Being too professional without a touch of friendliness is not therapy. We believe everyone deserves to be heard and to also live their best life. There is a plan for everyone.

e

Team Work

Individually, we do not know and teamwork makes a dream work. So each team member sticks to their areas of specialization.
