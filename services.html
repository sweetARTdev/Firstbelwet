THERAPY AND COUNSELLING

Psychological and evidenced-based actions to help you heal, become your best self and prevent a relapse. All you need to do is indicate where you need help and a specialist will be assigned to you immediately. The following are available therapy, counselling and coaching services:

Trauma-focused therapy

Trauma can be from sexual violence/rape, domestic violence, experiencing a gruesome accident, fire outbreak etc.,
An unhealed or buried trauma often resurfaces in the future to become a post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD). This degenerates to several level of depression and if urgent therapeutic help is neglected suicidal.thoughts, attempts and suicide is inevitable.

Therapy for diabetic, and hypertensive patients among others

Diabetics and high blood pressure among others are triggers to several psychological disorders. Also, psychological disorders are triggers to diabetics and high blood pressure among others. A diabetic specialist and a therapist professionally ensure that the individual heals faster and also engages in several coping mechanisms that will make the healing long-lasting

Personality assessments

A personality assessment makes you know your personality type and this makes you understand your temperament better, you understand why you do, think and act the way you do. Knowing your personality type makes you understand yourself more, maximize your strength and overcome your weaknesses. Knowing your personality type makes you understand people better. Take, for instance, there are traits in personality type A that will be a strength to people having a distressed personality, I.e personality type D. .
